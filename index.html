Common questions

What Most Riders Ask Before Booking

I already use TrainerRoad. Why isn't it working?

TrainerRoad has one of the best training algorithms on the market — and it's structurally blind to 5 biological signals that matter more for your progression at 40+ than the ones it can see. It's not broken. It just can't see you. That's the entire premise of this method.

I don't have time to add more training hours.

You won't. Every rider who's gone through Second Wind has maintained their current volume or reduced it. The method is about making the hours you already ride finally produce the adaptation your body is ready for — not stacking more stress on top of a system that's already over-reached.

Will this work at my age? I'm in my early fifties.

The Hidden Signal Method was specifically engineered for men between 40 and 60. The physiology is different at this stage — hormonal recovery, sleep architecture, life load. Every protocol in Second Wind accounts for that. Thomas was 52 and dropping riders 20 years younger inside 6 months.

My schedule is unpredictable. I travel constantly.

That's built into the system. When your signals tell us you're under-recovered from travel, we don't push intensity. The plan restructures around what your body can actually absorb that week — not what a static schedule says you should do.

I have an injury history. Can I still do this?

Bring it to the diagnostic call. We'll look at your history and be honest about whether Second Wind is the right fit. We don't take riders we don't think we can help.

How is this different from hiring a random coach on TrainingPeaks?

Most TrainingPeaks coaches build great plans. Very few read the 5 biological signals systematically every week and adjust training in real time around them. That specific method — not the plan design — is what's produced the gains we're seeing in men your age.

What time zones are available for calls?

We serve athletes in PT, CT, and ET. Our coaches schedule calls around your availability. Most of the work — plan adjustments, data review, signal reading — happens asynchronously, so you're not tied to a fixed weekly call time.
